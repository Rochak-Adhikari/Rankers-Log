To combat these vile beasts, ordinary people received superhuman powers and became known as "Hunters." Twenty-year-old Sung Jin-Woo is one such Hunter, but he is known as the "World's Weakest," owing to his pathetic power compared to even a meager E-Rank. Still, he hunts monsters tirelessly in low-rank Gates to pay for his mother's medical bills.
