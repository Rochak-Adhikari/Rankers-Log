The Game World (Introduction)

Welcome to Rankers Log. By accessing the System, you agree to abide by these protocols. The System provides tracking for anime, manga, and literary progression. This is a living digital ecosystem; your actions within the UI reflect your standing. Unauthorized manipulation of the DOM or injection of malicious scripts will result in immediate ejection from the server.

We reserve the right to update the "Game World" parameters (App Features) at any time. Significant patches (updates to these terms) will be broadcasted to your notification deck.

Player Conduct (Rules of Behavior)

Users must maintain civil communication channels. Harassment, hate speech, or griefing other users will result in immediate suspension of System privileges.

  • Do not impersonate System Administrators or Game Masters.
  • Do not exploit bugs or glitches for leaderboard advancement.
  • Respect the privacy of other Rankers. Doxxing is a permaban offense.

Inventory & Assets (Virtual Items)

All virtual items, badges, and achievements remain the property of Rankers Log. While you hold the license to use these assets on your profile, trading of account credentials or selling in-game items for real-world currency (RMT) is strictly prohibited.

warning Note: Assets lost due to unauthorized account sharing cannot be restored by Support.

System Termination (Banning Policy)

Administrators reserve the right to terminate access for any violation of these terms without prior notice. Appeals may be submitted via the Support Portal, but decisions regarding "Cheating" (use of bots/scripts) are final.
